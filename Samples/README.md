# Samples
## Compute
Applies a blur to a .png with a compute shader and presents it.
## Graphics
Rotating cubes with a texture, press the "T" key to toggle TAA off and on.
## Raytracing
Cubes and point lights placed randomly in the scene with direct lighting. Hold "A" to accumulate, and press "R" to toggle between the reference shader and a simple ReSTIR implementation.
